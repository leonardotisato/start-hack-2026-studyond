# Studyond Ideas

## 1. CV Tailoring Agent

An AI agent that analyzes a student's profile (skills, experience, education) and a target job posting, then automatically restructures and rewrites their CV to highlight the most relevant qualifications. It adapts tone, keyword density, and section ordering to match the employer's expectations and ATS requirements.

## 2. Cover Letter Generation

A guided cover letter generator that takes the tailored CV and job description as input, then produces a personalized cover letter. It maps the student's strongest experiences to the role's key requirements and maintains a professional yet authentic voice. Students can iterate on tone (formal, conversational) and emphasis.

## 3. Referral & Networking System

A matching engine that connects students with alumni, mentors, and professionals within partner companies. Based on shared interests, study programs, and career goals, it suggests warm introductions and facilitates referral requests. Includes reputation tracking so active mentors get recognized.

## 4. Thesis Research Agent

An AI-powered research assistant that helps students discover relevant thesis topics, find academic papers, and identify potential supervisors. It cross-references the university's active research areas, available datasets, and company-sponsored projects to suggest thesis opportunities that align with the student's interests and career direction.

## 5. Shared Planning Space

A collaborative workspace where student teams can co-plan semester schedules, group projects, and study sessions. Features include shared calendars, task boards, document collaboration, and integration with university course catalogs. Designed to reduce coordination overhead and keep groups aligned on deadlines and deliverables.
